About Us

Mow’s Dairy Farm is a family-run dairy based in Naya Raipur, Chhattisgarh, committed to delivering pure, nutritious, and responsibly produced dairy products. Our herd is raised on balanced, high-quality fodder, with routine veterinary care and humane handling practices to ensure animal welfare and consistent milk quality.

We follow strict hygiene and cold-chain protocols from milking to packaging. Every batch of milk undergoes quality checks for adulterants, fat/SNF levels, and freshness. Our product line includes fresh milk, cultured curd, traditional ghee, creamy butter, and soft paneer—each made in small batches to preserve taste, aroma, and nutrition.

Beyond products, we operate a transparent cow marketplace where responsible buyers and sellers can trade healthy dairy cows at fair prices. By improving genetics, nutrition, and housing, we help local farmers increase yield, reduce disease, and raise the bar for dairy in our region.

Our promise is simple: clean, honest dairy you can trust.
